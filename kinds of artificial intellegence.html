Narrow AI

Also referred to as weak AI or narrow AI, it is the only type of AI that has been successful so far.

Strong or General Artificial Intelligence

Artificial general intelligence, also referred to as strong, is the concept of a machine with general intelligence that simulates human intelligence or behaviors, with the ability to learn and apply its intelligence to solve any problem.

Super Artificial Intelligence

Artificial superintelligence or superintelligence, is a hypothetical artificial intelligence that does not only simulate or understand human intelligence and behavior, ASI is in which machines become self-aware and surpass the ability of human intelligence and ability.
